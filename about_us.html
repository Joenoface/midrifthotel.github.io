About Us

Located in the heart of Nakuru

Welcome to Mid-Rift Hotel, your ultimate destination for luxurious accommodation and mouth-watering dining experiences. Situated along Kenyatta Avenue, our establishment offers a perfect blend of comfort and taste, featuring hotel room bookings on the second floor and three exquisite dining outlets serving a variety of delicious foods. Whether you're here for business or leisure, we ensure your stay is pleasant and your meals unforgettable.

Our Dining Outlets

Lenz Pizza Joint

Enjoy a wide range of freshly made pizzas, from classic margherita to gourmet flavors that will leave you craving for more. Perfect for pizza lovers!

Mainstreet Barbecue

For burger enthusiasts, Mainstreet Barbecue offers succulent burgers packed with flavor. Our grilled dishes are prepared with the freshest ingredients.

Rosttoast Coffee Lounge

Relax and unwind at Rosttoast Coffee Lounge, where we serve expertly brewed coffee and delightful pastries. A perfect stop for a refreshing break.
